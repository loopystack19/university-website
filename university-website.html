Facilities at Northvale Institute of Technology

Our campus facilities empower every part of your journey—from modern classrooms and cutting-edge labs to welcoming study spaces and athletic venues. We emphasize accessibility, safety, and sustainability to support all students. Explore building hours, spaces to book, and the services that keep campus life running smoothly.

Library

The Northvale Institute of Technology Library provides access to technical books, research journals, and digital resources to support students and faculty in their academic pursuits.

Basketball Court

The basketball facility provides access to regulation courts, training spaces, and community programs to support players, coaches, and fans in their development and enjoyment of the sport.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria provides a variety of hot meals, grab-and-go options, and beverages to serve students, staff, and visitors.
